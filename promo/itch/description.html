Classic Sudoku — plus variant rules you can mix and match.

A clean, offline number puzzle. Play plain Sudoku, or switch on up to two variant rules and combine them for a fresh kind of grid. Every puzzle has a single solution and can be cracked by logic alone — no guessing — with difficulty graded by a real solver, not a random clue count.

Variants (pick up to two, roll them with Random, or play Classic)

  • Diagonal — both main diagonals must also hold 1–9
  • Hyper — four extra 3×3 boxes must also hold 1–9
  • Killer — dashed cages add up to the small number in their corner, with no digit repeated inside
  • Antiknight — cells a knight's move apart can't share a digit
  • Antiking — cells touching diagonally can't share a digit
  • Jigsaw — nine irregular regions replace the 3×3 boxes
  • Clone — two same-shaped regions hold the same digits, cell for cell
  • Even/Odd — shaded squares must hold an even digit, circles an odd one
  • Kropki — a white dot joins consecutive neighbours, a black dot two where one is double the other
  • XV — X joins two cells that add to 10, V two that add to 5
  • Thermo — digits rise along each thermometer, from the bulb to the tip
  • Palindrome — each marked line reads the same in both directions

How to play

  • Fill — tap a cell, then tap a number (or type 1–9)
  • Notes — pencil-mark candidates before you commit
  • Color — shade cells in any of nine colors to track your logic
  • Hint — reveals the next logical step: first the technique and region, then the exact cell
  • Undo & Erase — step back or clear a cell anytime
  • Auto-save — close it and pick up right where you left off

Plays with touch (mobile) or mouse & keyboard (desktop). Want it as an installable, fully offline app? Add it to your home screen from vatroslav.github.io/sudoku.
